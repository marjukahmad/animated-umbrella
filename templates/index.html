Lionel Messi Records

Lionel Messi is one of the greatest footballers of all time. Here are some of his notable records:

  • Most goals in a calendar year: 91 goals in 2012
  • Most goals in a single season in Europe's top five leagues: 50 goals in 2011-2012
  • Most hat-tricks in a calendar year: 8 hat-tricks in 2012
  • Most goals in a single UEFA Champions League season: 14 goals in 2011-2012
  • Most goals in a single UEFA Champions League match: 5 goals in 2012
  • Most goals in a single UEFA Champions League campaign: 16 goals in 2011-2012
  • Most goals in the UEFA Champions League overall: 115 goals
  • Most goals in a single La Liga season: 50 goals in 2010-2011
  • Most goals in a single La Liga match: 5 goals in 2012
  • Most goals in La Liga overall: 435 goals
